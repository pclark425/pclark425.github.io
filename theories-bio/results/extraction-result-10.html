algorithm_description Compute covariance matrix of mass-weighted Cartesian (or internal angle-transformed) coordinates from an ensemble/trajectory, diagonalize to obtain eigenvectors (principal modes) and eigenvalues, and project trajectories onto principal components q_i; free-energy profiles μ(q) = -k_BT ln P(q) constructed along low-index PCs to identify basins and folding pathways. For UNRES internal-angle PCA, angles are mapped via sine/cosine transform to avoid periodicity.
