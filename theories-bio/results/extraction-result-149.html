proposed_mechanism Loss-of-function of PARK2 (parkin), an E3 ubiquitin ligase, has distinct consequences depending on context: germline loss in neurons leads to neuronal dysfunction and Parkinson's disease, whereas somatic inactivation in non-neuronal dividing cells abrogates PARK2's tumor-suppressive activity.
