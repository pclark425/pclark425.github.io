Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-7

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-7

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of specific cell types in Alzheimer's disease that are described as susceptible or resilient, the evidence for this classification, and any proposed mechanisms or factors explaining their susceptibility or resilience.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
cell_type str The specific cell type discussed in the context of Alzheimer's disease (e.g., excitatory neuron, inhibitory neuron, astrocyte, microglia, oligodendrocyte, endothelial cell, etc.).
susceptibility_status str Is the cell type described as susceptible, resilient, or neither in the context of Alzheimer's disease? Use 'susceptible', 'resilient', 'neither', or 'unclear'.
evidence_for_status str A concise summary of the evidence supporting the susceptibility or resilience of this cell type (e.g., cell loss, molecular markers, resistance to amyloid/tau pathology, functional changes, etc.).
proposed_mechanisms str Any proposed mechanisms or factors that explain why this cell type is susceptible or resilient (e.g., gene expression profiles, protein aggregation, metabolic features, synaptic connectivity, protective pathways, etc.).
experimental_context str The experimental context in which the findings were made (e.g., human postmortem tissue, mouse model, in vitro, organoid, etc.).
disease_stage str The stage of Alzheimer's disease relevant to the findings (e.g., preclinical, early, late, unspecified).
contradictory_findings str Any reported contradictory findings or exceptions regarding the susceptibility or resilience of this cell type (e.g., studies reporting different results, context-dependent effects, etc.).
