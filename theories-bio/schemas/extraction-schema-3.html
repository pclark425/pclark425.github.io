Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-3

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-3

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of inverse correlations between cancer and neurodegenerative diseases, including the specific diseases involved, proposed molecular mechanisms, shared pathways or genes, and evidence supporting or challenging the inverse relationship.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
cancer_type str The specific type(s) of cancer discussed (e.g. 'lung cancer', 'breast cancer', 'melanoma', 'glioblastoma', etc.). Be specific.
neurodegenerative_disease str The specific neurodegenerative disease(s) discussed (e.g. 'Alzheimer's disease', 'Parkinson's disease', 'ALS', 'Huntington's disease', etc.). Be specific.
correlation_type str What is the nature of the correlation between the cancer and neurodegenerative disease? (e.g. 'inverse correlation', 'positive correlation', 'no correlation', 'mixed results'). Include quantitative measures if available.
epidemiological_evidence str What epidemiological evidence is provided for the correlation? Include statistics like odds ratios, relative risks, hazard ratios, prevalence rates, etc. Be specific and quantitative.
proposed_mechanism str What molecular or biological mechanism is proposed to explain the inverse correlation? Be detailed and specific (e.g. 'p53 hyperactivation leads to increased apoptosis in neurons but tumor suppression in dividing cells').
genes_involved str What specific genes are mentioned as having opposing or relevant roles in both cancer and neurodegeneration? List gene names and briefly describe their roles.
proteins_involved str What specific proteins are mentioned as having opposing or relevant roles in both cancer and neurodegeneration? List protein names and briefly describe their roles.
pathways_involved str What biological pathways are mentioned as being relevant to both diseases? (e.g. 'PI3K/AKT pathway', 'Wnt signaling', 'cell cycle regulation', 'apoptosis', 'autophagy', 'DNA repair', etc.)
cellular_process str What fundamental cellular processes are implicated in the inverse relationship? (e.g. 'cell proliferation vs cell death', 'cell cycle arrest', 'senescence', 'protein aggregation', 'oxidative stress response', etc.)
experimental_evidence str What experimental evidence supports the proposed mechanism? Describe the experiments and key findings briefly but specifically (e.g. 'knockout of gene X in mice increased cancer but decreased neurodegeneration').
counter_examples str Are there any counter-examples, exceptions, or cases where the inverse correlation does not hold? Or cases where both diseases co-occur?
study_type str What type of study is this? (e.g. 'epidemiological study', 'molecular biology study', 'genetic study', 'animal model study', 'computational study', 'review/meta-analysis', etc.)
key_finding str What is the key finding or conclusion of the paper regarding the inverse correlation between cancer and neurodegeneration? Be concise but information-dense.
