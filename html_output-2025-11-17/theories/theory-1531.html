Statement 1: Quantum Squeezing Enhances Negative Energy Confinement (qualitative)

ConditionDetails

• quantum field → is_prepared_in → squeezed vacuum state, and
• squeezing parameters → are_optimized_for → negative energy density
THEN
• spacetime region → can_achieve → sustained negative energy density suitable for FTL engineering

Supporting Evidence for this Law

  1. Quantum squeezing redistributes vacuum fluctuations, allowing for regions of negative energy density.
  2. Squeezed states are routinely generated in quantum optics and have been proposed for gravitational wave detection.
  3. Negative energy densities in squeezed states can be made arbitrarily large in principle, subject to quantum inequalities.
  4. Quantum inequalities limit the duration and magnitude of negative energy, but squeezing can optimize the tradeoff.
