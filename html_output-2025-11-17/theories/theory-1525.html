Name: Localized Spacetime Engineering via Quantum Vacuum Manipulation

Theory Query: Build a theory of how faster-than-light (FTL) spacecraft travel could be viable today using realistic scenarios and budgets.

New Predictions (Likely outcome)

  1. Arrays of engineered negative energy regions will produce measurable, cumulative spacetime distortions detectable by precision interferometry.
  2. Scaling up the number and coordination of micro-regions will increase the magnitude of local metric perturbations.

New Predictions (Unknown outcome/high-entropy)

  1. Sufficiently large and coordinated arrays will enable macroscopic, navigable spacetime distortions suitable for FTL travel.
  2. Unexpected quantum instabilities or feedback effects may arise when scaling to macroscopic arrays.
