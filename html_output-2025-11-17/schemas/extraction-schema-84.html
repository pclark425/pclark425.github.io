Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-84

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-84

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of faster-than-light (FTL) spacecraft travel concepts, their physical principles, technological readiness, cost estimates, engineering challenges, and any proposed realistic implementation scenarios or budgets.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
ftl_concept_name str The name of the faster-than-light (FTL) travel concept or approach discussed (e.g. 'Alcubierre drive', 'traversable wormhole', 'quantum tunneling', etc.).
concept_description str A brief description of the FTL concept, including how it is theorized to work.
physical_principles str The underlying physical principles or theories that the FTL concept relies on (e.g. general relativity, quantum mechanics, exotic matter, etc.).
technological_readiness_level str The current technological readiness level (TRL) of the concept, or a description of how close it is to experimental or practical realization (e.g. 'purely theoretical', 'laboratory experiment', 'engineering proposal', etc.).
required_materials_resources str Any specific materials, resources, or energy requirements mentioned as necessary for the concept (e.g. 'negative energy', 'exotic matter', 'antimatter', 'gigawatt power sources', etc.).
estimated_cost_budget str Any estimates of the cost or budget required to implement or test the concept, including units and context (e.g. 'estimated $10 billion for a prototype', 'requires resources beyond current global GDP', etc.).
engineering_challenges str A summary of the main engineering or technical challenges identified for the concept (e.g. 'stabilizing negative energy', 'containing exotic matter', 'energy generation', etc.).
experimental_results str Any reported experimental results, tests, or demonstrations related to the FTL concept (e.g. 'laboratory analog experiment', 'numerical simulation', 'no experimental results', etc.).
implementation_scenarios str Descriptions of any proposed realistic implementation scenarios, including mission profiles, spacecraft designs, or step-by-step plans.
key_limitations_counterarguments str Any key limitations, counterarguments, or critiques of the concept's viability, including physical, economic, or engineering obstacles.
real_world_projects_funding str Mentions of any real-world projects, organizations, or funding sources pursuing or supporting the FTL concept (e.g. NASA, DARPA, private companies, etc.).
