proposed_mechanistic_explanations Authors propose two plausible mechanisms at the 5p15.33 locus: (1) variation affecting TERT may alter telomerase expression/activity and telomere maintenance, facilitating carcinogenesis; (2) variation affecting CLPTM1L may alter apoptosis of lung cells under genotoxic (e.g., tobacco carcinogen) stress, modifying susceptibility.
