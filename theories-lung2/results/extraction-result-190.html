timing_relative_to_thymoma MG can present concurrently with thymoma; cases described across wide age range including infancy. Pre-thymectomy anti-AChR antibody levels were reported as a risk factor for post-thymectomy MG in one cited study.
effect_of_thymectomy Thymectomy in thymoma-associated MG is associated with clinical improvement and reduction of anti-AChR antibody titers in reported series; course of disease changes after resection according to cited studies.
