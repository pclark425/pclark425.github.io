Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-2

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-2

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of proposed or discovered molecular drivers, mechanisms, or pathways in lung adenocarcinomas lacking known RTK/RAS/RAF pathway alterations, including genetic, epigenetic, metabolic, or microenvironmental factors, as well as any supporting or refuting evidence.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_type str What kind of study was performed? (e.g., whole exome sequencing, RNA-seq, methylation profiling, proteomics, functional assays, etc.)
cohort_description str Brief description of the patient cohort, including number of cases and percentage of driver-negative cases.
driver_negative_definition str How did the study define 'driver-negative'? (e.g., which genes/pathways were tested and found negative)
proposed_driver str Name(s) of any proposed or discovered alternative driver(s) (gene, pathway, mechanism, etc.).
driver_type str Type of driver (e.g., genetic mutation, gene fusion, epigenetic alteration, metabolic change, microenvironmental factor, etc.).
evidence_summary str Brief summary of the evidence supporting the proposed driver (e.g., statistical association, functional validation, clinical correlation).
counter_evidence str Any evidence that challenges or refutes the proposed driver(s) (e.g., lack of functional effect, lack of recurrence, etc.).
model_or_hypothesis str Any proposed model or hypothesis for what drives these tumors in the absence of known drivers.
reference str Citation or PMID of the paper.
