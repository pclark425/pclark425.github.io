Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-10

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-10

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of autoimmune diseases in thymoma patients, including prevalence rates, proposed mechanisms, immunological findings, and clinical characteristics that explain the connection between thymomas and autoimmunity.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
autoimmune_disease_name str The specific name of the autoimmune disease associated with thymoma (e.g., 'myasthenia gravis', 'pure red cell aplasia', 'systemic lupus erythematosus', etc.)
autoimmune_disease_prevalence str What percentage or proportion of thymoma patients develop this autoimmune disease? Include numerical values with units (e.g., '30-50%', '15 out of 100 patients'). null if no information.
thymoma_type str What type/classification of thymoma is discussed? (e.g., 'type B2', 'type AB', 'WHO classification A/AB/B1/B2/B3', 'cortical', 'medullary'). null if not specified.
proposed_mechanism str What mechanism is proposed to explain why this thymoma leads to autoimmunity? Be detailed and specific (e.g., 'impaired negative selection of autoreactive T cells', 'production of anti-AChR antibodies by neoplastic thymic epithelial cells', 'loss of AIRE expression leading to escape of autoreactive T cells', etc.)
immunological_findings str What specific immunological findings support the mechanism? (e.g., 'increased autoreactive T cells in peripheral blood', 'presence of anti-titin antibodies', 'reduced regulatory T cell numbers', 'altered T cell receptor repertoire', 'defective central tolerance'). null if no information.
autoantibodies_present str What specific autoantibodies were detected in patients? (e.g., 'anti-acetylcholine receptor antibodies', 'anti-titin', 'anti-RyR', 'anti-striational antibodies'). null if no information.
timing_relative_to_thymoma str Does the autoimmune disease appear before, during, or after thymoma diagnosis/treatment? Be specific if timing information is provided. null if no information.
effect_of_thymectomy str What happens to the autoimmune disease after surgical removal of the thymoma (thymectomy)? (e.g., 'complete remission in 40% of patients', 'no change', 'improvement but not resolution'). null if no information.
patient_characteristics str What patient characteristics are associated with development of autoimmunity in thymoma patients? (e.g., 'younger age', 'female sex', 'specific HLA types', 'tumor stage'). null if no information.
thymic_structure_abnormalities str What structural or functional abnormalities of the thymus are noted? (e.g., 'loss of corticomedullary differentiation', 'reduced AIRE expression', 'abnormal thymic epithelial cell function', 'altered Hassall's corpuscles'). null if no information.
t_cell_characteristics str What characteristics of T cells are noted in these patients? (e.g., 'oligoclonal T cell expansion', 'reduced CD4+CD8+ thymocytes', 'altered CD4:CD8 ratio', 'skewed TCR repertoire'). null if no information.
comparison_with_without_autoimmunity bool Does the paper compare thymoma patients with autoimmunity to thymoma patients without autoimmunity? (true, false, or null for no information)
control_comparison str What differences are found between thymoma patients (with autoimmunity) versus controls (either healthy controls or thymoma patients without autoimmunity)? Be specific about immunological or clinical differences. null if no comparison made.
