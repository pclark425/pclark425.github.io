Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-170

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-170

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of proposed causes of Alzheimer's disease, supporting or refuting evidence for each cause, methods for detecting Alzheimer's disease (including biomarkers, imaging, cognitive tests, etc.), and the effectiveness of these detection methods (such as sensitivity, specificity, and stage of detection). Also extract any controversies, limitations, or counter-evidence related to causes or detection methods.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
cause_type str The type of proposed cause for Alzheimer's disease (e.g. genetic, molecular, environmental, lifestyle, vascular, etc.).
cause_description str A concise description of the proposed cause, including specific genes, proteins, pathways, or environmental factors implicated (e.g. 'APOE4 allele', 'amyloid-beta accumulation', 'tau pathology', 'chronic inflammation', 'head trauma', etc.).
evidence_for_cause str Summary of the evidence supporting this cause (e.g. study results, statistical associations, experimental findings, animal models, etc.).
evidence_against_cause str Summary of any evidence or arguments against this cause, or limitations/controversies (e.g. negative studies, lack of replication, conflicting findings, etc.).
detection_method_type str The type of detection method discussed (e.g. biomarker, neuroimaging, cognitive test, genetic screening, etc.).
detection_method_description str A brief description of the detection method, including what is measured and how (e.g. 'CSF amyloid-beta levels', 'PET imaging of tau', 'Mini-Mental State Examination', 'blood-based biomarkers', etc.).
detection_performance str Reported effectiveness of the detection method, including sensitivity, specificity, accuracy, and the stage of disease at which it is effective (e.g. 'sensitivity 85%, specificity 90% for early-stage AD').
study_type str The type of study providing the evidence (e.g. case-control, cohort, randomized controlled trial, meta-analysis, animal study, review, etc.).
study_population str Description of the study population (e.g. sample size, age range, demographics, clinical characteristics, animal model, etc.).
controversies_or_limitations str Any reported controversies, limitations, or unresolved questions regarding the cause or detection method (e.g. 'amyloid hypothesis challenged by failed clinical trials', 'biomarker not validated in diverse populations', etc.).
