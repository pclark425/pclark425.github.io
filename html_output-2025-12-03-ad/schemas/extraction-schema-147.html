Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
hypothesis_name str Name of the proposed causal mechanism (e.g., 'amyloid cascade', 'tau propagation', 'neuroinflammation', 'vascular dysfunction', 'metabolic dysfunction', 'microbiome alteration', 'genetic risk').
hypothesis_description str Brief description of the hypothesis and how it is thought to contribute to Alzheimer's disease.
supporting_evidence str Summary of evidence that supports the hypothesis (e.g., animal model results, epidemiological associations, molecular findings).
contradictory_evidence str Summary of evidence that challenges or contradicts the hypothesis.
risk_factor str Specific risk factor mentioned (e.g., APOE ε4 allele, hypertension, diabetes, age, smoking).
risk_factor_category str Category of the risk factor (genetic, vascular, metabolic, lifestyle, environmental).
detection_method str Name of the detection technique or biomarker (e.g., CSF Aβ42, plasma p‑tau181, amyloid PET, tau PET, MRI hippocampal volume, MMSE, digital gait analysis).
detection_method_type str Broad type of the detection method (fluid biomarker, imaging, cognitive test, digital/behavioral biomarker).
diagnostic_performance str Reported diagnostic metrics such as sensitivity, specificity, accuracy, AUC, odds ratio, with numeric values and units where applicable.
study_design str Design of the study providing the evidence (e.g., cross‑sectional, longitudinal cohort, case‑control, randomized trial).
sample_size int Number of participants or samples analyzed in the study.
population_characteristics str Key characteristics of the study population (e.g., age range, disease stage, clinical diagnosis criteria).
citation str Reference to the paper (title, authors, year, DOI) from which the information was extracted.
