Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-171

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of specific neuronal populations in the visual cortex that are reported to be selectively vulnerable or resistant to amyloid-β plaques and/or tau protein tangles in dementia, including the type of pathology, the neuronal population identity, evidence for vulnerability or resistance, proposed mechanisms or factors, disease context, and species or model studied.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
neuronal_population str The specific neuronal population in the visual cortex discussed (e.g. 'layer 3 pyramidal neurons', 'parvalbumin interneurons', 'V1 neurons', etc.).
vulnerability_status str Whether the neuronal population is reported as 'vulnerable', 'resistant', or 'intermediate' to amyloid-β plaques and/or tau tangles.
pathology_type str The type of pathology affecting the population (e.g. 'amyloid-β', 'tau', 'both', or 'other').
evidence_summary str A concise summary of the experimental or observational evidence supporting the vulnerability or resistance (e.g. 'increased tau tangles in layer 5 neurons', 'no amyloid-β detected in calretinin interneurons', 'cell loss observed', etc.).
proposed_mechanisms_factors str Any proposed mechanisms or factors underlying the selective vulnerability or resistance (e.g. 'differential expression of APP', 'synaptic connectivity', 'metabolic rate', 'calcium buffering', 'gene expression profiles', etc.).
disease_context str The disease context in which the findings were reported (e.g. 'Alzheimer's disease', 'frontotemporal dementia', 'Lewy body dementia', etc.).
species_or_model str The species or model system studied (e.g. 'human', 'mouse', 'rat', 'transgenic mouse model', etc.).
contradictory_findings str Any reported contradictory or counter-evidence regarding the vulnerability or resistance of the neuronal population (e.g. 'other studies found no difference', 'conflicting results in different models', etc.).
