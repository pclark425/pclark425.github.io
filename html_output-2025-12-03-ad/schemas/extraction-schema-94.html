Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-94

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of proposed causes or mechanisms of Alzheimer's disease, and any methods or biomarkers used for its detection, including evidence, results, and limitations.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
proposed_cause str A proposed cause or mechanism of Alzheimer's disease described in the paper (e.g., amyloid-beta accumulation, tau pathology, genetic mutations, inflammation, vascular factors, etc.).
cause_evidence str A concise summary of the evidence supporting or refuting the proposed cause, including key findings, study type (e.g., human, animal, in vitro), and any relevant statistics or results.
detection_method str A method or technique used to detect Alzheimer's disease (e.g., CSF biomarker, blood biomarker, PET imaging, MRI, cognitive test, etc.).
biomarker_or_finding str The specific biomarker, imaging finding, or clinical feature used for detection (e.g., amyloid-beta42 in CSF, tau PET signal, hippocampal atrophy on MRI, MMSE score, etc.).
detection_performance str Performance metrics of the detection method, such as sensitivity, specificity, accuracy, AUC, or other relevant statistics. Include numerical values and units if available.
detection_stage str The stage of Alzheimer's disease at which the detection method is effective (e.g., preclinical, mild cognitive impairment, dementia stage, etc.).
study_type str The type of study providing the evidence (e.g., human clinical study, animal model, in vitro, meta-analysis, review, etc.).
limitations_or_counter_evidence str Any limitations, challenges, or counter-evidence mentioned regarding the proposed cause or detection method (e.g., conflicting results, small sample size, lack of specificity, etc.).
