Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-118

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-118

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of large language models (LLMs) being used to distill qualitative laws, principles, or generalizable rules from large numbers of scholarly or scientific papers, including methods, results, limitations, and examples.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
llm_model_name str The name of the large language model (LLM) used for qualitative law extraction (e.g., GPT-4, Llama-2, PaLM, etc.).
llm_model_description str A brief description of the LLM, including size, architecture, or notable features relevant to the task.
task_goal str A concise description of the specific task or goal (e.g., 'extracting qualitative laws from physics papers', 'synthesizing general principles from biomedical literature').
domain str The scientific or scholarly domain(s) from which qualitative laws are being distilled (e.g., physics, biology, social sciences, multidisciplinary).
methodology str A brief summary of the methodology used to apply the LLM for law/principle extraction (e.g., prompt engineering, chain-of-thought, retrieval-augmented generation, human-in-the-loop, etc.).
type_of_qualitative_law str A description or example of the qualitative law, principle, or rule distilled by the LLM (e.g., 'laws of cell migration', 'principles of social network formation').
evaluation_metrics str The metrics or criteria used to evaluate the quality or correctness of the distilled qualitative laws (e.g., human expert agreement, novelty, coverage, accuracy, etc.).
results_summary str A concise summary of the results, including how well the LLM performed at distilling qualitative laws, and any quantitative or qualitative findings.
human_involvement str The extent of human involvement in the process (e.g., fully automated, human-in-the-loop, human evaluation only, etc.).
dataset_or_corpus str The name or description of the dataset or corpus of scholarly papers used as input for the LLM.
limitations_or_challenges str Any reported limitations, challenges, or failure cases encountered when using LLMs for qualitative law extraction.
notable_examples str Any notable or illustrative examples of qualitative laws or principles distilled by the LLM, as reported in the paper.
