reported_mechanism str Proposed biological mechanism(s) explaining vulnerability or resistance (e.g., 'high NMDA‑receptor expression', 'elevated oxidative stress', 'low mitochondrial density', 'enhanced autophagy', 'synaptic hyperactivity').
associated_genes_proteins str Genes, proteins, or molecular markers linked to the finding (e.g., 'APOE ε4', 'BACE1', 'tau phosphorylated at Ser202', 'GAD67', 'CaMKII').

disease_stage str Stage of dementia/AD referenced (e.g., 'early AD', 'Mild Cognitive Impairment', 'Braak stage III–IV').
counter_evidence str Any reported contradictory or null findings regarding the same neuronal population or region.
reference_citation str Short citation or DOI to identify the source paper.
