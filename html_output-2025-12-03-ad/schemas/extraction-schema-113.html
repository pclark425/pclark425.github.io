Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-113

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of faster-than-light (FTL) spacecraft travel concepts, including their names, descriptions, underlying physical principles, required technology, estimated costs or budgets, technological readiness, engineering or physical challenges, proposed solutions, experimental or simulation results, feasibility assessments, and any counterarguments or critiques.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
ftl_concept_name str The name of the faster-than-light (FTL) travel concept or approach discussed (e.g. 'Alcubierre drive', 'traversable wormhole', 'quantum tunneling', etc.).
concept_description str A concise description of the FTL concept, including how it is proposed to work.
physical_principles str The underlying physical principles or theories that the concept relies on (e.g. general relativity, quantum field theory, exotic matter, etc.).
required_technology str A list or description of the key technologies, materials, or components required to realize the concept (e.g. negative energy, high-energy lasers, advanced propulsion systems, etc.).
estimated_cost str Any estimates of the cost or budget required to develop or implement the concept, including units (e.g. USD, EUR, etc.), and whether the estimate is for a prototype, full-scale system, or other stage.
technological_readiness_level str The current technological readiness level (TRL) or stage of development for the concept (e.g. theoretical, laboratory experiment, prototype, engineering design, etc.).
engineering_challenges str A summary of the main engineering or physical challenges that must be overcome for the concept to be viable.
proposed_solutions str Any proposed solutions or approaches to address the identified challenges.
experimental_or_simulation_results str Any reported experimental or simulation results relevant to the concept, including outcomes and limitations.
feasibility_assessment str The authors' assessment of the overall feasibility of the concept, including technical, physical, and economic considerations.
counterarguments_or_critiques str Any counterarguments, critiques, or reasons why the concept may not be viable, as discussed in the paper.
real_world_projects_or_funding str Mentions of any real-world projects, organizations, or funding sources related to the development or study of the concept.
