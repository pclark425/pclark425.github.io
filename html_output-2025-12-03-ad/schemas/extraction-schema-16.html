Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-16

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-16

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of experimental methods, techniques, or procedures being transferred, adapted, or applied from one scientific domain or context to another scientific domain or context, including details about the transfer process and outcomes.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
procedure_name str The name of the experimental method, technique, protocol, or procedure being transferred (e.g. 'CRISPR gene editing', 'X-ray crystallography', 'machine learning classification', etc.)
procedure_description str A detailed description of the procedure, including what it does and how it works.
procedure_type str The type or category of procedure (e.g. 'measurement technique', 'analytical method', 'experimental protocol', 'computational method', 'data analysis technique', etc.)
source_domain str The original scientific domain or context where this procedure was developed or commonly used (e.g. 'molecular biology', 'physics', 'computer science', 'chemistry', etc.)
target_domain str The new scientific domain or context where this procedure is being applied or transferred to (e.g. 'neuroscience', 'materials science', 'ecology', etc.)
transfer_type str How was the procedure transferred? (e.g. 'direct application without modification', 'adapted/modified for new context', 'analogical transfer with significant changes', 'hybrid approach combining with existing methods', etc.)
modifications_made str What specific modifications, adaptations, or changes were made to the procedure for it to work in the new domain/context? Be specific about what was changed and why.
transfer_success str Was the transfer successful? Describe the outcome, including any quantitative metrics if available (e.g. 'successful - achieved 95% accuracy', 'partially successful - worked but with limitations', 'unsuccessful - failed due to X', etc.)
barriers_encountered str What barriers, challenges, or obstacles were encountered during the transfer process? (e.g. 'different scales of measurement', 'incompatible equipment', 'different theoretical assumptions', etc.)
facilitating_factors str What factors facilitated or enabled the successful transfer? (e.g. 'similar underlying principles', 'availability of analogous materials', 'collaborative expertise', etc.)
contextual_requirements str What contextual factors or requirements were necessary for the transfer to work? (e.g. 'required specialized equipment', 'needed domain expertise', 'required specific environmental conditions', etc.)
generalizability str Based on the paper's discussion, how generalizable is this transfer? Could the procedure be applied to other domains as well, or is it specific to this particular transfer?
knowledge_type str What type of knowledge was primarily transferred? (e.g. 'tacit/practical know-how', 'explicit procedural steps', 'theoretical principles', 'instrumental/technical skills', 'interpretive frameworks', etc.)
