Theory (General Information)

Name: Intrinsic Molecular Signature Theory

Type: general

Theory Query: Build a theory of why certain neuronal populations within the visual cortex are selectively vulnerable or resistant to amyloid-β plaques and tau protein tangles in dementia.

Description: This theory proposes that the selective vulnerability or resistance of visual cortical neuronal populations to amyloid-β and tau pathology is determined by their intrinsic molecular expression profiles, including genes related to calcium homeostasis, oxidative stress response, and synaptic plasticity.
