Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-3

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-3

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of the prevalence, types, and mechanisms of EGFR mutations in lung cancer across different populations, especially East Asians versus others, including proposed genetic, environmental, or lifestyle explanations for ethnic differences.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
population_studied str The population(s) or ethnic group(s) studied in the paper (e.g., East Asian, Caucasian, African, etc.).
sample_size int The number of subjects or samples included in the study.
egfr_mutation_prevalence str The reported prevalence or frequency of EGFR mutations in the studied population(s), ideally broken down by exon (18, 19, 20, 21) if available. Include units (e.g., %, n/N).
mutation_types str The specific types of EGFR mutations identified (e.g., exon 19 deletion, L858R in exon 21, etc.), and their frequencies if reported.
proposed_mechanisms str Any proposed mechanisms or explanations for the observed ethnic differences in EGFR mutation prevalence (e.g., genetic ancestry, founder effect, environmental exposures, lifestyle factors, etc.).
evidence_for_mechanisms str Summary of evidence supporting each proposed mechanism (e.g., genetic studies, environmental/lifestyle data, statistical associations).
evidence_against_mechanisms str Summary of evidence or arguments against proposed mechanisms, or null findings (e.g., studies that did not find a genetic basis, or that environmental factors do not explain the difference).
clinical_implications str Any reported clinical implications related to EGFR mutation prevalence differences (e.g., response rates to EGFR inhibitors by ethnicity, impact on treatment decisions).
study_design str Brief description of the study design (e.g., case-control, cohort, meta-analysis, population genetics study, etc.).
reference str Citation or identifier for the paper (e.g., DOI, PMID, or full citation).
