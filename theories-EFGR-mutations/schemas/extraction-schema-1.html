Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-1

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of EGFR mutation frequencies in lung cancer across different ethnic or ancestral populations, particularly comparing East Asian patients to other populations, including environmental factors, genetic background, and proposed mechanisms for population differences.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_population str Description of the study population, including sample size, ethnic/racial composition, and geographic location (e.g. '500 East Asian patients in Taiwan', '200 Caucasian patients in US', etc.)
population_ancestry str The specific ancestry or ethnic group being studied (e.g. 'East Asian', 'Chinese', 'Japanese', 'Korean', 'Caucasian', 'European', 'African', 'Hispanic', etc.)
egfr_mutation_frequency str The frequency or percentage of EGFR mutations in this population (e.g. '45%', '30 out of 100 patients', etc. Include confidence intervals if available)
specific_egfr_mutations str Specific EGFR mutations identified, particularly in exons 18, 19, 20, 21 (e.g. 'exon 19 deletions: 25%, L858R in exon 21: 15%', etc.)
smoking_status str The smoking status distribution in the study population (e.g. 'never-smokers: 60%, former smokers: 30%, current smokers: 10%', null if not reported)
egfr_mutation_smoking_association str Association between EGFR mutations and smoking status in this population (e.g. 'EGFR mutations more common in never-smokers (70% vs 10%)', null if not reported)
environmental_factors str Any environmental factors mentioned that might differ between populations (e.g. air pollution levels, occupational exposures, cooking oil fumes, secondhand smoke, etc.)
germline_genetic_factors str Any germline genetic variants, polymorphisms, or ancestry markers associated with EGFR mutation risk (e.g. specific SNPs, haplotypes, repair gene variants, etc.)
comparison_populations str If the study compares multiple populations, describe the comparison and key findings (e.g. 'East Asian 45% vs Caucasian 15%, p<0.001', null if no comparison)
proposed_mechanism str Any mechanisms proposed by the authors to explain why EGFR mutations are more common in certain populations (e.g. 'genetic susceptibility due to DNA repair variants', 'environmental carcinogen exposures', 'gene-environment interactions', etc.)
clinical_characteristics str Clinical characteristics of patients with EGFR mutations in this population (e.g. 'predominantly adenocarcinoma, non-smokers, female', etc.)
confounding_factors str Any confounding factors or alternative explanations mentioned (e.g. 'differences in histology distribution', 'screening biases', 'testing rates', etc.)
geographic_variation str Any geographic variations mentioned within or between populations (e.g. 'urban vs rural', 'China vs Japan vs Korea', etc.)
