Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-5

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of EGFR mutation prevalence in lung cancer across different ethnic populations, particularly comparing East Asian and non-Asian populations, including frequencies, patient characteristics, environmental factors, and proposed explanations for ethnic differences.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_population str Description of the study population, including sample size, ethnicity/ancestry (e.g., East Asian, Caucasian, African, mixed), and geographic location.
egfr_mutation_type str Specific EGFR mutations identified (e.g., exon 19 deletions, L858R in exon 21, G719X in exon 18, insertions in exon 20, T790M, etc.).
mutation_frequency_asian str Frequency/prevalence of EGFR mutations in East Asian patients (numerical percentage or fraction, null if no information).
mutation_frequency_non_asian str Frequency/prevalence of EGFR mutations in non-Asian patients (numerical percentage or fraction, specify ethnicity, null if no information).

lung_cancer_histology str Histological subtype of lung cancer associated with EGFR mutations (e.g., adenocarcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, etc.).
environmental_factors str Any environmental factors mentioned as potentially associated with EGFR mutations or ethnic differences (e.g., air pollution, cooking fumes, occupational exposures, tobacco types).
genetic_factors str Any genetic, ancestral, or germline factors mentioned as potentially explaining ethnic differences in EGFR mutation prevalence (e.g., genetic polymorphisms, ancestry markers, inherited susceptibility).
proposed_mechanism str Any proposed biological, molecular, or evolutionary mechanisms explaining why EGFR mutations are more common in Asian populations.
odds_ratio_or_risk str Statistical measures of association between ethnicity and EGFR mutations (e.g., odds ratio, relative risk, hazard ratio with confidence intervals, null if no information).
confounding_factors str Any confounding factors or alternative explanations discussed (e.g., differences in screening practices, referral bias, socioeconomic factors).
key_findings str Brief summary of the key findings related to ethnic differences in EGFR mutations, including any novel insights or hypotheses proposed by the authors.
