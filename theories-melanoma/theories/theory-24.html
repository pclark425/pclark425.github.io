New Predictions (Likely outcome)

  1. Digitized quantification of viable tumor plus immune-infiltrate metrics in the index node will further stratify relapse risk within MPR.

New Predictions (Unknown outcome/high-entropy)

  1. A single adjuvant PD-1 consolidation dose in MPR patients will not improve RFS but may increase toxicity; a non-inferiority trial could confirm omission.

Negative Experiments
