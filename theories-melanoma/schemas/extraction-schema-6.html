Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-6

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of the duration of anti-PD1 immunotherapy in melanoma, including how duration relates to degree of response (e.g., complete response, partial response, stable disease), criteria for discontinuation, outcomes by duration and response, and any recommendations or guidelines regarding optimal duration.
