Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-7

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-7

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of anti-PD-1 therapy duration strategies and outcomes in melanoma, including planned/actual duration, stopping rules, and whether optimal duration differs by depth of response (CR/PR/SD). Capture setting (metastatic vs adjuvant), regimen, response-stratified relapse and survival after stopping, comparisons of stopping vs continuing, biomarkers guiding discontinuation (e.g., PET/ctDNA), retreatment outcomes after relapse, toxicity with prolonged therapy, and any cost-effectiveness or modeling analyses. Prefer concise, numeric, and timepoint-specific results where available.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_identifier str Concise identifier for the study (e.g., first author + year, trial name; examples: 'KEYNOTE-006 2021 update', 'Dutch Melanoma Registry 2019', 'DANTE trial protocol').
paper_title str Full title of the paper.
year int Publication year (numeric if available).
doi_or_trial_id str DOI or clinical trial registration ID (e.g., NCT number), if reported.
study_type str Study design/type (e.g., randomized controlled trial, prospective cohort, retrospective registry, real-world, systematic review/meta-analysis, trial protocol, modeling/cost-effectiveness).
setting str Disease setting and line of therapy; specify 'metastatic/unresectable' or 'adjuvant' (stage), and first-line vs later-line if reported.
sample_size str Number of patients overall and number evaluable for discontinuation analyses (e.g., 'n=550 treated; n=185 discontinued electively').
therapy_regimen str Anti-PD-1 agent(s) and combination details (e.g., 'pembrolizumab 200 mg q3w monotherapy', 'nivolumab ± ipilimumab'). Include dosing if stated.
anti_pd1_monotherapy bool True if the study arm/data pertain to anti-PD-1 monotherapy only; false if combination or mixed; null if unclear.
duration_strategy str Planned/actual treatment duration policy (e.g., 'treat until progression/toxicity', 'cap at 2 years', 'stop at confirmed CR after ≥6 months', 'response-adapted stop', 'PET- or ctDNA-guided stop').
stopping_criteria str Explicit criteria to discontinue (e.g., 'RECIST v1.1 confirmed CR on 2 scans ≥4 weeks apart', 'complete metabolic response on PET', 'grade ≥3 irAE', 'patient choice').
median_time_on_therapy str Time on therapy before discontinuation (median and range/IQR; include units, e.g., 'median 11.2 months [IQR 7–17]').
response_at_discontinuation str Distribution of best response at the time of stopping (e.g., 'CR 48%, PR 35%, SD 17%'; specify response criteria such as RECIST/iRECIST if given).
has_response_stratified_outcomes bool True if outcomes after stopping are reported separately for CR vs PR vs SD; false otherwise.
outcomes_by_response str Outcomes after discontinuation stratified by CR/PR/SD (relapse/progression rates, PFS/OS/TFS at specified timepoints, medians, HRs). Provide numeric values with units and timepoints (e.g., '12-mo relapse: CR 8%, PR 28%, SD 45%').
compare_stop_vs_continue str If the study compares elective stopping vs continuing therapy (randomized or observational), summarize arms and key effect sizes (HRs with 95% CIs, absolute risk differences) for PFS/OS/relapse.
recurrence_rate_after_stop str Overall relapse/progression after elective discontinuation (e.g., cumulative incidence at 12/24/36 months, median time to relapse). Include numbers and units.
treatment_free_survival str Treatment-free survival metrics after stopping (medians, proportions at timepoints, definitions used).
re_treatment_outcomes str Outcomes of retreatment/rechallenge after relapse (response rate, disease control rate, PFS/OS upon rechallenge; include regimen and numeric results).
toxicity_vs_duration str Immune-related adverse events in relation to treatment duration (incidence of late irAEs, grade ≥3 events, discontinuation due to toxicity; any correlation with longer duration).
biomarkers_guiding_stop str Biomarkers used to guide discontinuation (e.g., PET complete metabolic response, ctDNA negativity) and their predictive performance for relapse (e.g., NPV/PPV, HRs).
cost_effectiveness str Any economic/cost-effectiveness or modeling analyses comparing durations (e.g., 1 year vs 2 years): costs, QALYs, ICERs, optimal duration estimates.
follow_up_after_stop str Duration of follow-up after discontinuation (median, range) and minimum required follow-up for inclusion in analyses.
authors_recommendation str Authors' explicit recommendations or conclusions on optimal duration, including whether it should differ by response category.
key_limitations str Important limitations/biases impacting interpretation (e.g., selection bias in elective discontinuation, immortal time bias, small numbers in SD subgroup).
country_or_registry str Geographic context or data source (e.g., Netherlands DMTR, MSKCC, Flatiron, multicenter international).
notes str Any other relevant details (e.g., inclusion/exclusion of brain metastases, BRAF status effects, imaging schedules, definitions used).
