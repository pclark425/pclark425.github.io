Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-3

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-3

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of clinical trials and translational studies in resectable melanoma that compare neoadjuvant-plus-adjuvant immunotherapy with adjuvant-only immunotherapy, or that provide mechanistic evidence for why neoadjuvant exposure improves outcomes. Capture study design, patient population, regimens and timing, surgical feasibility, pathologic response, survival endpoints (HRs and absolute rates), immune correlates (e.g., TCR expansion, TIL infiltration, interferon signatures, antigen spreading), safety, subgroup effects, limitations, and authors' mechanistic claims or counterevidence.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_title str Full title of the paper or trial report (e.g., 'Neoadjuvant Pembrolizumab in Resectable Stage III Melanoma').
trial_name_or_id str Named trial or registration ID (e.g., SWOG S1801, OpACIN, OpACIN-neo, PRADO, NCT number).
citation str First author and journal reference in short form (e.g., 'Amaria et al., NEJM 2022').
year int Publication year.
study_design str Design and phase (e.g., 'Phase II randomized', 'Phase III RCT', 'single-arm translational').
has_direct_comparison bool Does the study directly compare neoadjuvant-plus-adjuvant versus adjuvant-only immunotherapy in melanoma? (true/false)
arms_description str Concise description of study arms (e.g., 'neoadjuvant pembrolizumab x3 then surgery + adjuvant pembro vs surgery then adjuvant pembro').
patient_population str Population description (e.g., 'resectable stage IIIB–IIID/IV melanoma'; mucosal/acral included/excluded).
stage_criteria str Disease stage and resectability criteria used for enrollment.
sample_size_total int Total number of patients enrolled.
sample_size_per_arm str Number of patients per arm (e.g., 'neo+adj n=154; adj-only n=159').
intervention_neoadjuvant str Neoadjuvant regimen, drugs, doses (e.g., 'pembrolizumab 200 mg q3w x3').
neoadjuvant_cycles_and_timing str Number of cycles and timing relative to surgery (e.g., '2 cycles over 6 weeks; surgery at week 6').
intervention_adjuvant str Postoperative adjuvant regimen in the neoadjuvant arm (e.g., 'pembro for up to 1 year').
comparator_adjuvant_only str Adjuvant-only regimen in the control arm (e.g., 'adjuvant pembro 1 year').
time_to_surgery str Timing metrics around surgery (median time from start to surgery; any protocol-defined windows).
surgery_feasibility_missed_surgery str Rates of on-time surgery, delays, downstaging, missed resections, or conversion to unresectable.
pathologic_response_definition str Definitions used (e.g., pCR=0% viable tumor; MPR=≤10% viable).
pcr_rate_neoadjuvant str Pathologic complete response rate in neoadjuvant-treated surgical specimens (include n/N and %).
mpr_rate_neoadjuvant str Major pathologic response rate (include definition and %).
association_path_response_with_survival str Association between pathologic response (pCR/MPR) and EFS/RFS/OS (e.g., HRs, Kaplan–Meier separation).
clinical_endpoints_reported str Which endpoints were reported (EFS, RFS, OS, DMFS), including timepoints.
event_free_survival str EFS results comparing arms (HR with CI/p-value; absolute % at specific timepoints).
recurrence_free_survival str RFS results comparing arms (HR with CI/p-value; absolute % at timepoints).
overall_survival str OS results comparing arms (HR with CI/p-value; absolute % at timepoints).
distant_metastasis_free_survival str DMFS results comparing arms (HR with CI/p-value; absolute % at timepoints).
hazard_ratios_and_CIs str All reported HRs with 95% CIs for primary/secondary endpoints.
absolute_benefit_timepoints str Absolute differences at prespecified timepoints (e.g., 2-yr EFS +12%).
follow_up_duration_median str Median follow-up duration and IQR/range if available.
immune_correlates_summary str High-level summary of mechanistic/immune findings relevant to neoadjuvant benefit.
peripheral_immune_findings str Peripheral blood immune changes (e.g., expansion of tumor-reactive TCR clones, cytokines).
intratumoral_immune_findings str Tumor microenvironment changes (e.g., increased CD8+ TILs, IFN-γ gene signatures, PD-L1 upregulation).
tcr_repertoire_changes str Evidence for TCR clonality/diversity changes and trafficking between tumor and blood.
antigen_spreading_or_epitope_spreading str Evidence for antigen/epitope spreading after neoadjuvant therapy.
biomarker_predictors str Baseline or on-treatment biomarkers associated with benefit (e.g., PD-L1, TMB, gene signatures).
mechanism_claims_by_authors str Authors' stated mechanistic rationale for why neoadjuvant improves outcomes (concise quotes or summaries).
safety_grade3plus_irAE str Rates of grade ≥3 immune-related AEs per arm; include specific toxicities if reported.
treatment_related_deaths str Any treatment-related mortality and context.
adjuvant_discontinuation_rate str Rates of adjuvant discontinuation due to AEs in each arm.
surgical_complications_or_delay str Surgical morbidity, wound complications, and delay rates attributable to neoadjuvant therapy.
subgroup_analyses str Subgroup results (e.g., by stage, PD-L1 status, BRAF status) and interaction p-values if reported.
recurrence_patterns str Patterns of failure (local/regional vs distant) by arm.
resistance_mechanisms str Proposed or observed mechanisms of resistance in the neoadjuvant context.
counterevidence_or_null_results str Findings that challenge the benefit of neoadjuvant therapy or show no advantage vs adjuvant-only.
limitations_and_biases str Key limitations (sample size, early stopping, imbalance, follow-up duration) and potential biases.
funding_and_conflicts str Funding sources and conflicts of interest.
trial_registration str Clinical trial registration (e.g., NCT number) and registry link if available.
