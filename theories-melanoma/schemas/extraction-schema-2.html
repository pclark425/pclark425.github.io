Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-2

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-2

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of studies comparing neoadjuvant-adjuvant immunotherapy to adjuvant only immunotherapy in melanoma, including clinical outcomes, mechanistic explanations, and any reported reasons for differences in efficacy.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_type str Type of study (e.g. clinical trial, preclinical study, retrospective analysis, review, meta-analysis).
patient_population str Description of the patient population studied, including melanoma stage and relevant inclusion criteria.
immunotherapy_regimen str Details of the immunotherapy regimens used, including agents, dosing, timing (neoadjuvant, adjuvant, both), and duration.
outcomes_measured str Clinical outcomes measured (e.g. overall survival, disease-free survival, recurrence rate, pathological complete response, event-free survival, immune-related adverse events).
comparative_results str Summary of comparative results between neoadjuvant-adjuvant and adjuvant only immunotherapy (e.g. hazard ratios, response rates, statistical significance, effect sizes).
mechanistic_findings str Mechanistic or biological findings explaining differences in outcomes (e.g. immune cell infiltration, T cell expansion, tumor microenvironment changes, antigen presentation, immune memory).
reported_reasons_for_improvement str Any explicit explanations or hypotheses provided in the paper for why neoadjuvant-adjuvant immunotherapy improves outcomes compared to adjuvant only.
limitations_or_counter_evidence str Any reported limitations, conflicting results, or counter-evidence regarding the benefit of neoadjuvant-adjuvant immunotherapy over adjuvant only.
