Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-4

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of anti-PD1 immunotherapy duration for melanoma patients, including treatment schedules, outcomes by response category, and comparisons of different duration strategies.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_name str The name or identifier of the clinical study or trial (e.g., 'KEYNOTE-006', 'CheckMate 067', or first author name if no formal trial name).
anti_pd1_agent str The specific anti-PD1 agent used (e.g., 'pembrolizumab', 'nivolumab', 'cemiplimab').
melanoma_setting str The clinical setting of melanoma treatment (e.g., 'metastatic/advanced', 'adjuvant stage III', 'adjuvant stage IV', 'neoadjuvant').
treatment_duration_protocol str The planned or protocol-specified duration of anti-PD1 therapy (e.g., 'until progression or toxicity', 'fixed 1 year', 'fixed 2 years', '4 doses', 'indefinite').
actual_median_duration str The actual median duration of treatment received by patients, if reported (e.g., '8.3 months', '12 months'). Include units. Null if not reported.
response_categories_assessed str What response categories were evaluated? (e.g., 'CR, PR, SD, PD', 'complete response vs partial response', 'responders vs non-responders').
duration_differs_by_response bool Does the study report different treatment durations or stopping strategies based on degree of response? (true, false, or null for no information)
response_adapted_strategy str If duration differs by response, describe the response-adapted treatment strategy (e.g., 'CR patients stopped at 1 year', 'continued until progression regardless of response', 'early stoppers after CR').
efficacy_by_duration str Summary of efficacy outcomes by treatment duration. Include specific metrics like overall survival (OS), progression-free survival (PFS), response rates with numerical values and duration comparisons. Be detailed and information-dense.
efficacy_by_response_category str Summary of efficacy outcomes stratified by response category (CR, PR, SD, etc.). Include survival metrics, relapse rates, or durability of response with numerical values. Null if not reported.
outcomes_after_stopping str For patients who stopped therapy (especially those with CR or PR), what were the outcomes? Include relapse rates, duration of remission, or need for retreatment with numerical values. Null if not reported.
safety_by_duration str Summary of toxicity or adverse events related to treatment duration (e.g., 'grade 3-4 adverse events in 40% at 2 years', 'immune-related adverse events increased with longer duration'). Include percentages. Null if not reported.
discontinuation_rate_and_reasons str What percentage of patients discontinued treatment and why? (e.g., '30% discontinued due to toxicity', '15% discontinued after achieving CR', '25% due to progression'). Null if not reported.
comparison_of_durations bool Does the study explicitly compare different treatment duration strategies (e.g., 1 year vs 2 years, fixed duration vs until progression)? (true, false, or null)
optimal_duration_conclusion str What conclusion does the study reach about optimal treatment duration, if any? Include any recommendations about stopping rules or response-adapted strategies. Null if no conclusion stated.
