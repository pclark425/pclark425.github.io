Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-3

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of the role of older age groups (older children, adolescents, adults) in poliovirus transmission in Pakistan, including evidence for or against their involvement, study types, age group definitions, key findings, vaccination status, and any interventions or counterpoints.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_type str The type of study (e.g., epidemiological, seroprevalence, modeling, outbreak investigation, surveillance, case-control, etc.).
age_groups_studied str The specific age groups analyzed in the study, with definitions (e.g., 'adolescents 10-19 years', 'adults 20+ years', 'children 0-5 years').
role_of_older_age_groups str A concise summary of the evidence for or against the involvement of older age groups (older children, adolescents, adults) in poliovirus transmission, as reported in the study.
key_findings str The main findings relevant to the role of older age groups in transmission, including quantitative data if available (e.g., seroprevalence rates, secondary attack rates, proportion of cases, viral shedding data, etc.).
vaccination_status_by_age str Information on poliovirus vaccination status or coverage by age group, if reported.
interventions_targeting_older_ages str Any mention of interventions (e.g., vaccination campaigns, surveillance) specifically targeting older age groups, and their outcomes if available.
counter_evidence_or_limitations str Any evidence or arguments presented that older age groups do NOT play a significant role in transmission, or limitations of the study regarding age group analysis.
geographic_scope str The specific geographic area(s) in Pakistan covered by the study (e.g., national, province, district, city).
citation str The full citation of the paper (authors, year, title, journal).
