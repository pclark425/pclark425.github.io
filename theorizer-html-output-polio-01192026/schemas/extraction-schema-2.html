Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-2

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of the role of older age groups (\u22655 years, adolescents, and adults) in poliovirus transmission in Pakistan, including age-specific susceptibility/immunity, shedding and mucosal immunity, incidence and case age distribution, contact/mobility patterns, vaccination history and campaign targets/coverage, environmental surveillance findings, and modeling estimates of contribution to transmission. Capture quantitative results (percentages, rates, odds ratios, R/FOI, coverage) with units and age bands where available, plus any counterevidence or limitations.
