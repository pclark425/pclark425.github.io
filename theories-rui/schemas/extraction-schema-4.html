Extraction Schema Details for extraction-schema-4

Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-4

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of factors, mechanisms, or conditions that determine how single cells organize into tissues, including experimental findings about what promotes, inhibits, or regulates tissue organization.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
tissue_type str The type of tissue being studied (e.g. 'epithelial tissue', 'neural tissue', 'cardiac tissue', 'liver organoid', etc.)
experimental_system str The experimental system used to study tissue organization (e.g. 'mouse embryo', 'human iPSC-derived organoids', 'in vitro cell culture', 'zebrafish development', etc.)
cell_types_involved str What specific cell types are involved in the tissue organization process? List all relevant cell types mentioned.
factor_name str The name of a specific factor or mechanism that determines tissue organization (e.g. 'E-cadherin', 'Wnt signaling', 'mechanical tension', 'differential adhesion', etc.)
factor_category str The category of the factor (e.g. 'cell adhesion molecule', 'signaling pathway', 'mechanical force', 'transcription factor', 'extracellular matrix component', 'morphogen gradient', etc.)
factor_description str A detailed description of how this factor influences tissue organization. What does it do? How does it work?
factor_effect str What is the effect of this factor on tissue organization? Does it promote, inhibit, or regulate specific aspects? Be specific about the organizational outcome.
perturbation_method str If the factor was experimentally perturbed, how was it done? (e.g. 'knockout', 'knockdown', 'overexpression', 'inhibitor treatment', 'mechanical manipulation', etc. null if no perturbation)
perturbation_result str What happened to tissue organization when the factor was perturbed? Be specific and quantitative if possible. (null if no perturbation)
quantitative_measurements str Any quantitative measurements related to tissue organization (e.g. 'cell sorting efficiency increased by 40%', 'tissue size was 200 micrometers', 'expression increased 3-fold', etc. null if no quantitative data)
spatial_temporal_aspects str Are there specific spatial patterns or temporal dynamics mentioned? (e.g. 'gradient from anterior to posterior', 'occurs at day 3 of differentiation', 'oscillates with 2-hour period', etc.)
interactions_with_other_factors str Does this factor interact with or depend on other factors for tissue organization? Describe any mentioned interactions, dependencies, or combinatorial effects.
physical_vs_biochemical str Is this factor primarily physical/mechanical or biochemical/molecular in nature? Or both?
sufficiency_necessity str Is this factor described as necessary, sufficient, both, or neither for tissue organization? What does the evidence suggest?
