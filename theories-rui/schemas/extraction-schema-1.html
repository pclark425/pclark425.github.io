Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-1

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of algorithmic and deterministic approaches to protein folding, including physics-based methods, energy minimization algorithms, force field approaches, and rule-based folding methods.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
algorithm_name str The name of the protein folding algorithm or method being described.
algorithm_description str A detailed description of how the algorithm works, including its key steps and computational approach.
algorithm_type str The category or type of algorithm (e.g., 'molecular dynamics', 'Monte Carlo sampling', 'energy minimization', 'knowledge-based', 'ab initio', 'homology modeling', 'fragment assembly', 'constraint-based', etc.)
is_deterministic bool Is the algorithm deterministic (produces same output given same input) or stochastic? (true for deterministic, false for stochastic, null if no information)
underlying_principles str What are the underlying physical or computational principles used by the algorithm? (e.g., 'Anfinsen's principle', 'thermodynamic hypothesis', 'free energy minimization', 'statistical potentials', 'first principles physics', etc.)
force_field_or_energy_function str What force field, energy function, or scoring function does the algorithm use? (e.g., 'CHARMM', 'AMBER', 'Rosetta energy function', 'GROMOS', 'statistical potentials', etc.)
computational_complexity str What is the computational complexity or runtime of the algorithm? Include specific complexity notation if available (e.g., 'O(n^3)', 'exponential', 'polynomial time') or practical runtime information.
protein_types_tested str What types or sizes of proteins was the algorithm tested on? (e.g., 'small peptides (<50 residues)', 'medium proteins (50-150 residues)', 'large proteins (>150 residues)', 'membrane proteins', 'multi-domain proteins', etc.)
performance_metrics str What performance metrics were reported? Include specific numerical results with units (e.g., 'RMSD of 2.5 Ã…', 'TM-score of 0.75', 'GDT-TS of 65', 'success rate of 40%', etc.). Be as detailed as possible.
benchmark_datasets str What benchmark datasets or protein sets were used to evaluate the algorithm? (e.g., 'CASP targets', 'PDB structures', 'specific protein families', etc.)
requires_templates bool Does the algorithm require template structures or homologous proteins to work? (true, false, or null for no information)
is_interpretable bool Is the algorithm interpretable/explainable in terms of physical principles or rules, as opposed to being a black-box? (true, false, or null for no information)
key_assumptions str What are the key assumptions or simplifications made by the algorithm? (e.g., 'assumes native state is global energy minimum', 'ignores solvent explicitly', 'uses simplified representation', etc.)
limitations str What are the reported limitations or failure modes of the algorithm? Be specific about what types of proteins or scenarios it struggles with.
comparison_with_other_methods str If the paper compares this algorithm with other methods (including ML methods like AlphaFold), what are the key comparative results? Include specific performance differences.
