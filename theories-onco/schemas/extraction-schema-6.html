Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-6

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of EGFR mutation prevalence in lung cancer across different ethnic populations, particularly comparing Asian and non-Asian populations, and any proposed explanations for ethnic differences.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
study_population_ethnicity str The ethnicity or ethnic group(s) of the study population (e.g., 'East Asian', 'Caucasian', 'Japanese', 'Chinese', 'Korean', 'non-Asian', 'mixed', etc.)
study_population_location str Geographic location where the study was conducted (e.g., 'Japan', 'China', 'United States', 'Europe', etc.)
sample_size str The number of lung cancer patients included in the study for EGFR mutation analysis
egfr_mutation_frequency str The frequency or prevalence of EGFR mutations in the study population, expressed as percentage or proportion. Include specific exon information if available (e.g., '45% overall', '30% exon 19 deletions', '15% exon 21 L858R')
specific_mutation_types str Specific EGFR mutations detected, particularly in exons 18, 19, 20, 21 (e.g., 'exon 19 deletions', 'L858R in exon 21', 'G719X in exon 18', 'T790M in exon 20')
comparison_with_other_ethnicities bool Does the study directly compare EGFR mutation frequencies between different ethnic groups? (true/false/null)
comparative_frequency_data str If comparisons are made, what are the specific mutation frequencies in different ethnic groups? (e.g., 'Asian: 50%, Caucasian: 15%')
smoking_status_correlation str What is the relationship between EGFR mutations and smoking status in this population? (e.g., 'more common in never-smokers', 'no significant difference', etc.)
gender_correlation str What is the relationship between EGFR mutations and gender in this population? (e.g., 'more common in females', 'no significant difference', etc.)
proposed_explanation_genetic str Does the paper propose genetic or hereditary factors to explain ethnic differences in EGFR mutation prevalence? If yes, describe the proposed mechanism.
proposed_explanation_environmental str Does the paper propose environmental factors (e.g., air pollution, cooking practices, occupational exposures) to explain ethnic differences? If yes, describe the proposed mechanism.
proposed_explanation_lifestyle str Does the paper propose lifestyle factors (e.g., diet, smoking patterns) to explain ethnic differences? If yes, describe the proposed mechanism.
proposed_explanation_other str Does the paper propose any other explanations (e.g., detection bias, healthcare access, molecular subtypes) for ethnic differences? If yes, describe them.
lung_cancer_histology str What histological subtype of lung cancer is being studied? (e.g., 'adenocarcinoma', 'squamous cell carcinoma', 'NSCLC', 'mixed')
response_to_egfr_inhibitors str If mentioned, what is the response rate to EGFR tyrosine kinase inhibitors in this population? Include ethnicity if specified.
