Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-4

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of population or ethnic differences in EGFR-activating mutation prevalence in lung cancer and the proposed reasons for higher rates in East Asians, including germline genetic risk, environmental exposures, mutational processes, and demographic covariates; capture effect sizes, mutation spectra, and study details.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
paper_title str Full title of the paper.
year int Publication year.
doi_or_pmid str DOI or PMID of the study, if reported.
study_design str Study type (e.g., sequencing cohort, case-control, cohort, meta-analysis, GWAS, admixture study, environmental epidemiology).

mutation_detection_method str Method used to detect EGFR mutations (e.g., Sanger, ARMS-PCR, qPCR, NGS panel, WES/WGS) and sensitivity.
egfr_mutation_prevalence_overall str Overall prevalence of activating EGFR mutations (include numerator/denominator and %).
egfr_mutation_prevalence_by_ethnicity str Prevalence by ethnic group (e.g., East Asian vs non-Asian), with counts/percentages.
egfr_mutation_prevalence_by_covariates str Prevalence stratified by smoking status, sex, histology, age, or region (include numbers/percents).
egfr_mutation_subtypes_distribution str Distribution of EGFR mutation subtypes (e.g., exon 19 deletion, L858R, exon 20 insertion, G719X, T790M; include %).
adjusted_association_ethnicity str Adjusted association between Asian/East Asian ethnicity and EGFR-mutant status (e.g., OR/RR with 95% CI, p-value, covariates adjusted).
ancestry_analysis str Any admixture/ancestry proportion analyses linking genetic ancestry to EGFR mutation probability (effect sizes, p-values).
germline_risk_variants_associated str Germline variants/loci associated with EGFR-mutant lung cancer (gene, rsID, locus, risk allele, OR, p-value; note if association is specific to EGFR-mutant, never-smoker, sex-specific; include allele frequencies by population if reported).
regulatory_variants_in_EGFR str Evidence for regulatory germline variants affecting EGFR expression (e.g., intron 1 CA-SSR1 repeat length, promoter/enhancer SNPs) and their population frequency differences.
mutational_signatures_TMB str Tumor mutational burden and mutational signatures (e.g., APOBEC, aging, tobacco) associated with EGFR-mutant tumors and their prevalence by ethnicity.
environmental_exposures_associated str Environmental/occupational exposures linked to EGFR-mutant lung cancer (e.g., cooking oil fumes, biomass/coal, radon, second-hand smoke, PM2.5, indoor air pollution) with effect sizes and whether enriched in East Asian settings.
hormonal_or_biologic_factors str Evidence for hormonal or biologic factors (e.g., estrogen signaling, sex differences) influencing EGFR mutation risk and any ethnicity-specific data.
testing_or_selection_bias str Notes on diagnostic era, testing method sensitivity, referral patterns, or other biases that could influence observed prevalence differences.
authors_proposed_mechanisms str Concise summary of mechanisms proposed by authors to explain higher EGFR mutation rates in East Asians (categorize as germline, environmental, mutational process, demographic, or bias).
counter_evidence_or_null_results str Findings that contradict common explanations or show no association after adjustment (include statistics).
key_statistics str Most important quantitative results relevant to the question (e.g., ORs, HRs, CIs, p-values, absolute % differences).
